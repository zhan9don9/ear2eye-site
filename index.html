DIY: Build & Test

  1. Cut copper and steel foils with factory edges; pair to form a slit.
  2. Shine a safe laser through a single slit; capture images on paper/card.
  3. Flip the slit; compare left/right asymmetry. Photograph both.
  4. Write down setup (distance, laser type), then share images.

Safety first. Use low-power lasers and proper eye protection.
